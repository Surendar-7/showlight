WHO WE ARE

Crafting Experiences That Leave a Lasting Impact

At Showlights Entertainment LLP, we go beyond event execution to design immersive, experience-led brand moments that connect, engage, and deliver measurable business impact. Founded in 1997, we are a full-service experiential marketing and event management company specializing in Corporate Events, MICE Conferences, Product Launches, Plant Inaugurations, Brand Activations, Employee Engagement, Dealer Meets and Large Scale Business Experiences. With operations across South India and Pan India, we partner with leading organizations to create memorable brand experiences.
